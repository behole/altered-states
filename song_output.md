--- STYLE ---
70 BPM, wet Rhodes piano drenched in reverb with tape wobble, a single soft kick drum pulse that breathes with the vocal, and atmospheric synth pads that swell and recede like tides. Vocal is close-miked, whispered-near-breaking, half-spoken and half-sung — think James Blake meets Bon Iver's quieter moments. The mix leaves enormous empty space in the midrange; the silence is the hook. Emotional arc builds through restraint — the chorus should feel like almost-overflowing, the bridge like something cracking open, the outro like finally exhaling.
