The ayahuasca dossier currently has fewer peer-reviewed sources than the other substances. The following studies should be added to bring it up to parity:

- [ ] **Palhano-Fontes et al. (2019)** — "Rapid antidepressant effects of the psychedelic ayahuasca in treatment-resistant depression" — *Psychological Medicine*. fMRI + clinical outcomes; key evidence for ayahuasca's rapid antidepressant mechanism.
- [ ] **Sanches et al. (2016)** — "Antidepressant effects of a single dose of ayahuasca in patients with recurrent depression" — *Psychological Medicine*. Preliminary trial establishing ayahuasca's antidepressant potential.
- [ ] **Hamill et al. (2019)** — "Ayahuasca: Psychological and physiologic effects, pharmacological profile, and therapeutic use" — *The American Journal of Drug and Alcohol Abuse*. Comprehensive review of ayahuasca's psychological and physiological effects.
- [ ] **Bouso et al. (2012)** — "Personality, psychopathology, life attitudes and neuropsychological performance among ritual users of ayahuasca" — *PLoS ONE*. Long-term study of ritual ayahuasca users; key data on psychological safety profile.
