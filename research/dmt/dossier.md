## 1. Overview & Mechanism of Action

DMT (N,N-Dimethyltryptamine) is an endogenous tryptamine — naturally produced in the human brain, CSF, blood, and lung tissue. It is the only classic psychedelic that the human body manufactures.

**Two radically different routes, one molecule:**
- **Smoked/Vaporized/IV:** Onset in SECONDS. Duration 10-20 min. Complete reality replacement. "The rocket."
- **Ayahuasca (oral + MAOI):** Onset in 30-60 min. Duration 4-6 hours. Narrative, pedagogical, ceremonial. "The teacher plant."

**Mechanism of action:**

| Target | Activity | Significance |
|---|---|---|
| **5-HT2A** | Primary agonist | Shared with psilocybin/LSD; drives visual hallucinations |
| **5-HT2C, 5-HT1A** | Agonist | Modulates intensity and mood |
| **Sigma-1 receptor** | Agonist | **UNIQUE to DMT** — cellular stress response, neuroprotection, may explain NDE parallels and "alien" quality |
| **Trace amine receptors (TAAR)** | Agonist | **UNIQUE/prominent** — DMT IS a trace amine |
| **Endogenous production** | Human body manufactures DMT | Only classic psychedelic that is a naturally occurring human neuromodulator |

**Neural signature** [Sources 3, 4: Timmermann]:
- HIGHEST neural signal diversity (entropy) of any psychedelic measured
- Simultaneous DMN dissolution + global hyperconnectivity
- Brain activity resembles REM sleep — yet experience is "more real than real" (the paradox)
- Changes within SECONDS of administration

## 2. The 7 Phenomenological Dimensions — SMOKED/VAPORIZED DMT

### Dimension 1: Perceptual — COMPLETE REPLACEMENT

This is NOT augmentation of reality. This is wholesale transport to another place.

**Sub-breakthrough (10-20mg smoked):**
- Geometric patterns: intense, colorful, mathematically complex
- The "chrysanthemum" — rotating mandala of interlocking geometric forms
- "The waiting room" — an intermediate geometric space, sense of being assessed
- Room still visible but heavily overlaid with patterns
- Colors become impossibly vivid — "colors that don't exist"

**Breakthrough (30-50mg+ smoked):**
- **COMPLETE REPLACEMENT of sensory reality** — you are no longer in the room
- Transport to a self-consistent, immersive alien dimension [Source 7: Michael]
- The space: vast, luminous, geometric, crystalline, technological, architecturally complex
- Entity encounters — autonomous beings populating this space (see Section on Entities)
- Auditory: high-pitched buzzing/ringing at onset → complex alien sounds/language
- **"More real than real"** — the experience feels MORE vivid, detailed, and fundamentally real than ordinary waking consciousness [Sources 3, 5, 6]
- Impossible colors, hyperdimensional geometry, self-transforming objects/beings

**The breakthrough sequence** [Source 7: Michael]:
1. Initial visual distortion (seconds)
2. Geometric tunnel / chrysanthemum pattern
3. **"Breaking through"** — piercing a membrane, shooting through a portal
4. Immersion in alien hyperreal space
5. Entity contact
6. Return (rapid, "landing back in body")

---

### Dimension 2: Temporal — THE BREVITY PARADOX

**Smoked/IV:**
- Clock time: 10-20 minutes (smoked), 15-30 minutes (IV)
- Subjective time: hours, lifetimes, eternity
- The most EXTREME time distortion of any psychedelic
- Minutes feel like eons; the entire experience may feel like a lifetime
- Time concept itself may become meaningless — "outside of time"
- The brevity is both defining and disorienting — "the most profound experience of your life lasted 12 minutes"

**Onset timing:**
- Smoked: first effects in 5-15 SECONDS, breakthrough by 30-60 seconds
- IV: effects within 30-60 seconds, peak at 2-5 minutes [Source 1: Strassman]
- No other psychedelic acts this fast — there is NO gradual build

---

### Dimension 3: Cognitive — THE DOWNLOAD

**Sub-breakthrough:**
- Thought acceleration — processing feels infinitely faster
- Sense of approaching something vast and significant
- "I can't believe this is happening" quality

**Breakthrough:**
- Sense of receiving vast amounts of structured information — "the download"
- Information feels alien, non-human, cosmically significant
- Too complex to retain or articulate upon return
- Paradoxical clarity despite overwhelming intensity — "I could think perfectly clearly, I just couldn't believe what I was thinking"
- Noetic quality: direct encounter with "truth" or "fundamental reality"
- 96% of entity encounter respondents believed entities were conscious/intelligent beings [Source 6]
- Many report receiving specific "messages" or "teachings" — 69% in Davis survey
- The cognitive experience often feels like encountering an alien intelligence, not generating internal insights

---

### Dimension 4: Emotional — AWE AND TERROR

**The emotional arc of smoked DMT:**
1. **Pre-dose:** Anticipatory fear/excitement — "you always forget how intense it is"
2. **Onset (0-30s):** Shock, terror, "too much too fast," feeling of actual death
3. **Breakthrough (30s-2min):** Terror gives way to awe, wonder, bewilderment, or sustained terror
4. **Peak (2-10min):** Awe, cosmic love, alien wonder, gratitude, fascination — OR — sustained terror, confusion, feeling of being trapped
5. **Return (10-20min):** Profound gratitude, bewilderment, laughter, tears, "what was THAT"

**Emotional character:**
- More ALIEN than emotional — the feeling tone is often non-human, strange, otherworldly
- Love when present has a cosmic/alien quality — not interpersonal (MDMA) or personal (psilocybin) but VAST
- Terror is a real and common component (~25-30% experience significant fear)
- Awe is the dominant emotion — but it's awe-at-the-incomprehensible, not comfortable wonder
- Emotional INTENSITY exceeds all other psychedelics due to speed and totality

---

### Dimension 5: Self / Ego — INSTANTANEOUS DISSOLUTION

**Sub-breakthrough:**
- Self intact but feels very small against vast geometric space
- Ego awareness maintained: "I am here seeing this"

**Breakthrough:**
- **Complete ego dissolution within SECONDS** — not gradual like psilocybin/LSD
- "I" disappears; becomes "pure awareness" or "a point of consciousness"
- Identity may reconstruct in the alien space — "I was me, but somewhere else entirely"
- No time to prepare, resist, or surrender — it just HAPPENS
- The speed makes it MORE disorienting than gradual psychedelic ego death
- All 13 subjects in Timmermann 2018 scored above NDE threshold on Greyson Scale [Source 5]
- DMN dissolution more complete and rapid than any other psychedelic [Source 4]

### Dimension 6: Relational / Entity Contact — DMT'S DEFINING FEATURE

Entity encounters are NOT an edge case. They are the CORE of the DMT experience.

**Entity prevalence:** 50-75% of breakthrough doses produce entity contact [Sources 1, 2, 6]

**Entity typology** [compiled across all sources]:

| Type | Character | Source |
|---|---|---|
| "Machine elves" / jeweled beings | Playful, mischievous, creating objects from language | McKenna, Strassman |
| Insectoid / mantis beings | Clinical, procedural, "operating" on the user | Strassman, Davis |
| Serpents | Wisdom, sometimes threatening; cosmic serpent | Shanon (ayahuasca) |
| Guides / teachers | Benevolent, teaching, showing | Davis |
| Geometric / abstract entities | Sentient geometry, beings MADE of pattern | Strassman |
| "The Goddess" / feminine presence | Nurturing but stern; "Mother Ayahuasca" | Ayahuasca tradition |
| Jesters / clowns | Trickster energy, mocking ego, playful/unsettling | Strassman |

**Entity characteristics:**
- **Autonomous** — behave independently, not controlled by the user
- **Intelligent** — display purposeful, complex behavior
- **Intentional** — seem to have agendas: teaching, showing, healing, testing, playing
- **Emotionally warm** — many display joy at the user's arrival, as if EXPECTED
- **Communicative** — telepathic (non-verbal), show rather than tell
- **Perceived as REAL** — 72% continue to believe entities are independently real after the experience [Source 6]

**The "waiting room":**
- An intermediate space between normal reality and the breakthrough dimension
- Often described as a geometric, domed space with intricate patterns
- Sense of being "assessed" or "held" before being allowed through
- Entities may be peripheral here — "gatekeepers"
- Unique to DMT — no parallel in other psychedelic phenomenology

---

### Dimension 7: Somatic — THE LAUNCH

**Onset (smoked):**
- **"Blast-off" / "Launch"** — physical sensation of extreme acceleration
- Vibration throughout the body — like being plugged into an electrical current
- High-pitched buzzing/ringing in ears
- Pressure — as if being pushed through a very small space very fast
- Heart rate and blood pressure spike rapidly [Source 1]
- Pupil dilation (maximum)

**During the experience:**
- Body awareness DISAPPEARS at breakthrough — you have no body
- At sub-breakthrough: body feels strange, heavy, vibrating, electric
- The body experience is one of DISSOLUTION — not enhancement (MDMA) or heaviness (psilocybin) or buzzing (LSD)

**Return:**
- "Landing" back in the body — sudden reappearance of physical sensation
- Often accompanied by deep breaths, laughter, or tears
- Body feels normal within minutes
- Residual tingling/buzz for 15-30 minutes

## 3. Ayahuasca — The Extended Form

Ayahuasca is oral DMT (from Psychotria viridis) combined with beta-carboline MAO inhibitors (from Banisteriopsis caapi) that prevent first-pass metabolism.

**Same molecule. Completely different experience.**

| Feature | Smoked/IV DMT | Ayahuasca |
|---|---|---|
| **Onset** | 5-60 seconds | 30-60 minutes |
| **Duration** | 10-20 minutes | 4-6 hours |
| **Visual character** | Complete replacement, alien, technological | Narrative, autobiographical, mythological, organic |
| **Entity character** | Machine elves, insects, aliens | Serpents, jaguars, ancestors, spirits, "Mother Ayahuasca" |
| **Cognitive quality** | Overwhelming download | Reflective, contemplative, pedagogical |
| **Ego dissolution** | Instantaneous, total | Gradual, partial-to-complete |
| **Emotional tone** | Awe/terror, alien wonder | Cathartic, reverent, grief + love |
| **Body** | Launch → dissolution | Heavy, nauseous, purging |
| **Purging** | None | Central feature (~50%) — la purga |
| **Setting** | Anywhere (brief) | Ceremonial, often with shaman/facilitator |

**Ayahuasca staged progression** [Source 9: Shanon]:
1. Somatic: nausea, heaviness, stomach discomfort
2. Geometric: abstract patterns, colors
3. Figurative: recognizable forms — serpents, jaguars, faces
4. Narrative: full scenes, stories, autobiographical visions
5. Mystical/noetic: cosmic truths, divine encounters, profound acceptance

**Ayahuasca's purge (la purga):**
- Vomiting and/or diarrhea in ~50% of sessions
- NOT a side effect — experienced as physical and spiritual cleansing
- "Purging negativity" — emotional/spiritual material being expelled through the body
- Often precedes the deepest visionary content

## 5. Challenging / Difficult Experience Dimension

DMT terror is qualitatively different from other psychedelic challenges:

- **Feeling of actual death** — not metaphorical; genuine conviction of dying
- **Alien abduction quality** — on a table, probed by insectoid beings, in a clinical/technological environment [Source 2: Strassman]
- **Malevolent entities** — beings that probe, test, mock, or overwhelm
- **Loss of ALL identity** — complete ego death with no reference point, no self, no memory of self
- **The "too real" problem** — terror arising because the experience is TOO real to dismiss
- **Infinite loops** — trapped in recursive patterns with no exit
- **Speed of onset** — no time to prepare, adjust, or surrender; it just HAPPENS

**The saving grace:** Smoked DMT is BRIEF. Even the most terrifying experience resolves in 10-20 minutes. This makes DMT paradoxically both the most extreme AND the most manageable psychedelic.

**Ayahuasca challenging experiences:** Can last HOURS. More like psilocybin's emotional shadow — grief, fear, confrontation with personal material. But resolved by the pedagogical quality: "Mother Ayahuasca is showing you this for a reason."

## 6. Linguistic and Cognitive Fingerprint (for Prompt Design)

**Sub-breakthrough — linguistic markers:**
- "Oh. Oh wow. Oh—" — exclamatory, overwhelmed, brief
- Geometric language: fractal, crystalline, mandala, rotating, infinite
- "Impossible" constructions: "colors that don't exist," "shapes that can't be"
- Acceleration quality in language — everything speeding up

**Breakthrough — linguistic markers:**
- Language FAILS almost immediately — far more than with psilocybin or LSD
- When attempted: short, declarative, awestruck: "I was there. They were there. They knew me."
- Entity language: "they," "them," "the beings," "it showed me," "they were waiting"
- The word "real" repeated: "it was real, it was more real than this"
- Spatial language: "I was SOMEWHERE ELSE," "I went somewhere," "another place entirely"
- Technological/alien descriptors: "crystalline," "domed," "machine-like," "hyperspace"
- Post-return: bewildered, struggling, laughing, crying, "I can't... I can't even..."
- The "fading" quality: "it's already slipping away, I can't hold it"

**Ayahuasca — linguistic markers:**
- More narrative, flowing, story-like than smoked DMT
- Nature imagery: serpent, jaguar, river, forest, roots, vine
- Ancestral language: "my grandmother was there," "I saw my lineage"
- Teaching quality: "it showed me," "I was given to understand," "the medicine told me"
- Purging language: "I had to let it go," "it came up and out of me"
- Ceremonial quality: reverent, grateful, humble

## 7. Distinguishing Features vs. Other Substances

| Feature | DMT (Smoked) | DMT (Ayahuasca) | Psilocybin | LSD | MDMA |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Duration** | 10-20 min | 4-6 hours | 4-6 hours | 8-12 hours | 3-5 hours |
| **Onset** | SECONDS | 30-60 min | 20-40 min | 30-60 min | 20-45 min |
| **Character** | Alien, explosive, hyperreal, "the rocket" | Pedagogical, narrative, ceremonial, "the teacher plant" | Organic, earthy, introspective, "the teacher" | Electric, analytical, cosmic, "the technician" | Warm, empathic, direct, "the connector" |
| **Ego dissolution** | Instantaneous, total | Gradual, partial-to-complete | Gradual, oceanic | Gradual, expansive | Softening only |
| **Visual world** | COMPLETE REPLACEMENT | Narrative visions, eyes-open distortion | Overlay/breathing | Geometric overlay | Minimal |
| **Entities** | Core feature (50-75%) | Common (serpents, spirits, ancestors) | Rare | Very rare | None |
| **Hyperreal quality** | "More real than real" | "Deeply meaningful" | "Altered/dreamlike" | "Electric/enhanced" | "Normal but FEELS different" |
| **Emotional tone** | Awe, terror, alien wonder | Reverence, catharsis, teaching | Grief + love, catharsis | Analytical wonder | Love, empathy, warmth |
| **Body** | Launch → dissolution | Heavy, purging, nausea | Heavy, earthy | Electric, buzzing | Warm, stimulated |
| **Challenging quality** | Alien terror, feeling of death, brief | Emotional confrontation, purging, hours | Emotional overwhelm | Thought loops, trapped | Comedown (delayed) |
| **Post-experience** | "What WAS that?" — bewilderment | Integration, gratitude, teaching | Emotional integration | Cognitive insights | Afterglow → comedown |

**DMT's distinctive quality (smoked):** "The rocket" — the most extreme altered state available. No gradual build, no time to adjust, no partial experiences at breakthrough doses. You are HERE, then you are THERE, then you are BACK. The "there" is an alien dimension populated by autonomous intelligences that feels MORE real than this reality. Nothing else in psychedelic phenomenology matches the speed, totality, otherness, and hyperreal quality of breakthrough DMT.
