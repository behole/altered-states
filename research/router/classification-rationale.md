# Router — Classification Rationale

> **Project:** Altered States Skill Project
> **Last updated:** 2026-04-05
> **Purpose:** Document the phenomenological and pharmacological basis for routing heuristics

---

## Why These Routes?

The router maps user intent to substance based on phenomenological character. These mappings are grounded in the research dossiers and reflect the PRIMARY dimension each substance operates in.

### Emotional depth / introspection → Psilocybin

**Basis:** Psilocybin's primary phenomenological dimension is emotional/mystical. Griffiths 2006/2016 demonstrated that psilocybin reliably produces mystical experiences with deep emotional significance — 67% rated it a top-5 life experience. Barrett 2022 confirmed the emotional/cathartic quality is the primary experiential signature. The 5D-ASC "oceanic boundlessness" factor (emotional openness, ego softening, connectedness) loads highest for psilocybin.

**When this routes:** "I need to process something," "I want to go inward," "emotional," "introspective," generic "trip" or "journey" requests.

### Analytical clarity / structural insight → LSD

**Basis:** LSD's D2 dopamine partial agonism (Passie 2008) drives its distinctive analytical, energized quality — absent in psilocybin. Carhart-Harris 2016 showed LSD increases global brain connectivity with maintained cognitive drive (REBUS + dopaminergic exploration). Holze 2022 confirmed the LSD-specific "electric/analytical" character as a tonal overlay driven by receptor profile. Kettner 2021 phenomenological comparison found LSD users reported more "insight" and "analytical" descriptors.

**When this routes:** "I want to see the structure," "analytical," "cosmic perspective," "geometric," "architecture."

### Warmth / connection / empathy → MDMA

**Basis:** MDMA is pharmacologically distinct — serotonin RELEASER (not receptor agonist), plus oxytocin release (Hysek/Liechti 2012). Nichols 1986 coined "entactogen" specifically to distinguish from psychedelics: MDMA produces emotional opening without perceptual/cognitive restructuring. Mithoefer 2018 and Mitchell 2021 demonstrated MDMA's therapeutic mechanism is fear reduction (amygdala dampening) + empathic connection.

**When this routes:** "I need to feel safe," "connection," "warmth," "empathy," "I want to talk about something hard without being afraid."

### Extreme intensity / alien / overwhelming → DMT (smoked)

**Basis:** DMT produces the highest neural signal diversity (entropy) of any psychedelic measured (Timmermann 2019). Sigma-1 receptor agonism (unique to DMT, Frecska 2016) may explain the "alien" quality and NDE parallels. Complete reality replacement, entity encounters in 50-75% of breakthrough doses (Davis/Griffiths 2020), "more real than real" quality. Nothing else matches the speed, totality, and otherness of breakthrough DMT.

**When this routes:** "Blast me off," "hyperspace," "entities," "the most intense," "alien."

### Ceremonial / narrative / ancestral → Ayahuasca

**Basis:** Same molecule as DMT but radically different phenomenology via oral route + MAOI (Riba 2001/2003). Extended duration (4-6 hours) enables narrative, autobiographical, pedagogical content impossible in 12-minute smoked window. Shanon 2002 documented cross-cultural content consistency (serpents, jaguars, palaces) and staged progression (somatic → geometric → figurative → narrative → mystical). "Mother Ayahuasca" as teaching presence.

**When this routes:** "Ayahuasca," "ceremony," "the medicine," "the vine," "plant medicine," "la purga."

### Default: Psilocybin Medium

**Basis:** Psilocybin has the broadest phenomenological profile — it touches all 7 dimensions without being extreme in any single one. It's the most studied (Griffiths 2006/2008/2016, Barrett 2022). When no substance is specified and no clear thematic cue is present, psilocybin medium provides the most balanced, accessible altered state.

## Blend Routing Rationale

When users request combinations, route to the DOMINANT substance and carry the secondary as a tonal overlay:

| Blend | Dominant | Secondary Overlay | Rationale |
|---|---|---|---|
| Candy flip (LSD + MDMA) | LSD | MDMA's empathic warmth | LSD's perceptual/cognitive restructuring dominates; MDMA adds heart |
| Hippy flip (Psilocybin + MDMA) | Psilocybin | MDMA's direct warmth, fear reduction | Psilocybin's emotional depth dominates; MDMA softens the difficulty |
| Pharmahuasca | Ayahuasca | (none — this IS ayahuasca) | Synthetic replication of ayahuasca via oral DMT + pharmaceutical MAOI |

**Why dominant + overlay, not true blends:** Each skill is designed as a coherent phenomenological voice. Blending two skills 50/50 would produce an incoherent hybrid that matches neither substance. The dominant + tonal overlay approach preserves the primary skill's integrity while acknowledging the secondary influence.
