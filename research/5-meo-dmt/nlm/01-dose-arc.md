The dose arc of a smoked or vaporized 5-MeO-DMT experience is highly compressed, typically lasting a total of **2 to 20 minutes**, with a median duration of about 15 minutes [1, 2].

**Onset (Seconds)**
The onset is exceptionally fast, occurring **within seconds** of inhalation [1, 3]. Driven by rapid brain penetration, users experience an intense physical body load and a profound somatic sensation of dying or releasing [3, 4]. This abrupt transition can initially trigger acute anxiety, confusion, or panic, but these emotional spikes typically resolve very rapidly as the user surrenders [4-6]. 

**Peak (2 to 3 Minutes)**
Peak plasma levels and maximal subjective effects are reached in just **2 to 3 minutes** [3]. During this peak phase, the intense physical onset gives way to **complete and immediate ego dissolution** [4, 5]. Rather than the immersive visionary environments seen with N,N-DMT, the 5-MeO-DMT peak is defined by "formless mysticism"—a pure consciousness event where the user feels they are dissolving into a blinding white light, the "void," or achieving complete unity with a formless source [1, 2, 4, 7]. 

**Return (15 to 30 Minutes)**
The return to normal waking consciousness is incredibly swift, with the bulk of the acute experience concluding within **15 to 30 minutes** [2, 8]. Unlike other psychedelics where realizations unfold gradually during a long comedown, the return from 5-MeO-DMT is characterized by **immediate integration** [4]. Acute cognitive alterations, such as impaired working memory and cognitive flexibility, typically fully recover within roughly **30 minutes post-peak** [5].

**Afterglow and Sustained Effects**
While the acute drug effect is remarkably brief, the psychological afterglow can be long-lasting. Users frequently report that the intensity of the peak mystical experience mediates **sustained improvements in depression, anxiety, and stress**, with profound positive benefits still recorded at 30-day follow-ups [7].
