## 1. Overview & Mechanism of Action

MDMA (3,4-methylenedioxymethamphetamine) is an **entactogen** — a pharmacologically distinct class from classic psychedelics. The term "entactogen" (coined by Nichols, 1986) means "touching within" — producing a state of emotional openness, empathy, and contact with authentic inner experience.

**MDMA is NOT a psychedelic.** Despite colloquial grouping, it works through a fundamentally different mechanism:

| Feature | MDMA (Entactogen) | Classic Psychedelics (LSD/Psilocybin) |
|---|---|---|
| **Mechanism** | Monoamine transporter REVERSAL — releases stored serotonin, dopamine, norepinephrine | 5-HT2A receptor AGONISM — directly activates serotonin receptors |
| **Serotonin** | Massively RELEASED from vesicles (flooding the synapse) | Receptor STIMULATED (stores untouched) |
| **Consequence** | Depletion → comedown | No depletion → afterglow |

**Primary mechanisms** [Sources 3, 5]:
1. **SERT reversal:** Floods synapse with serotonin → empathy, warmth, emotional openness, fear reduction (~80% of subjective effects)
2. **Oxytocin release:** Hypothalamic oxytocin surge → bonding, trust, enhanced touch pleasure [Source 4]
3. **DAT reversal:** Dopamine release → euphoria, motivation, reward, stimulation
4. **NET reversal:** Norepinephrine release → arousal, alertness, sympathomimetic effects (HR, BP, temperature)
5. **Amygdala dampening:** Paradoxically increases cortisol while DECREASING amygdala reactivity to threat → high arousal + low fear = ideal for trauma processing

**What MDMA does NOT do:**
- Does NOT directly agonize 5-HT2A (the psychedelic receptor)
- Does NOT substitute for LSD in animal drug discrimination assays
- Does NOT produce hallucinations at standard doses
- Does NOT produce ego dissolution

## 2. The 7 Phenomenological Dimensions

### Dimension 1: Perceptual — MINIMAL

MDMA produces the **least perceptual change** of any substance in this project.

**Low intensity (75mg):**
- Slight brightening of colors
- Music sounds marginally richer
- Minimal visual change — the world looks normal

**Medium intensity (100-125mg):**
- Colors slightly more vivid
- Music perception enhanced emotionally (not acoustically restructured)
- Enhanced brightness and warmth of lighting
- Tactile sensitivity enhanced — touch becomes pleasurable, electric
- NO geometric patterns, NO synesthesia, NO complex imagery
- Faces appear softer, warmer, more beautiful (emotional projection, not perceptual distortion)

**High intensity (150mg+):**
- Slight visual blurring at very high doses
- Nystagmus (involuntary eye movement) may blur vision
- Late-session: MDA metabolite accumulation may produce very mild perceptual shifts
- Still NO hallucinations, NO geometric patterns, NO visionary content

**Key distinction:** MDMA's perceptual profile on the 5D-ASC Visionary Restructuralization scale is ~1/3 to 1/4 of psilocybin at comparable subjective intensities [Source 5]. The world looks basically NORMAL but FEELS profoundly different.

---

### Dimension 2: Temporal — MILD

**All intensities:**
- Minimal time distortion compared to psychedelics
- Present-moment focus — reduced rumination about past, reduced anxiety about future
- A sense of "being fully here" without the radical temporal dissolution of LSD/psilocybin
- Temporal orientation remains intact — you know what time it is, how long it's been
- Time may feel slightly stretched during peak emotional moments, but this is emotional density, not temporal distortion

**Key distinction:** MDMA does not produce the timelessness, eternity, or temporal transcendence characteristic of classic psychedelics. Time passes normally; you're just more present in it.

---

### Dimension 3: Cognitive — PRESERVED (with emotional enhancement)

**Low intensity:**
- Slightly enhanced verbal fluency
- Increased desire to communicate, share, talk
- Reduced cognitive avoidance of difficult emotional material
- Mild difficulty with working memory at tasks (but enhanced emotional processing)

**Medium intensity:**
- Full cognitive coherence maintained — this is definitional for the entactogen class [Source 3]
- Can narrate experiences, hold conversations, process memories
- Reduced rumination and cognitive avoidance
- Enhanced emotional insight — seeing emotional patterns clearly
- Increased self-compassion and reduced self-criticism in thinking about oneself
- Ability to revisit traumatic material WITHOUT being overwhelmed [Sources 1, 2]
- The "window of tolerance" expands — material normally avoided becomes accessible

**High intensity:**
- Some working memory impairment
- Increased talkativeness — difficulty stopping verbal flow
- Emotional insight remains strong
- Cognitive coherence maintained even at high doses (unlike psychedelics)

**Key distinction:** MDMA insight is EMOTIONAL and RELATIONAL, not cosmic or philosophical. "I can see how this pattern in my relationships connects to my childhood" — not "I can see the architecture of reality." Preserved cognitive coherence is the therapeutic mechanism — you can DO THERAPY during the session. [Sources 1, 2, 3]

---

### Dimension 4: Emotional — **DOMINANT DIMENSION**

This is MDMA's primary phenomenological territory. Everything else is secondary.

**Low intensity (75mg):**
- Mood elevation, warmth, well-being
- Increased openness and desire for social contact
- Subtle reduction in social anxiety
- Enhanced appreciation for people, music, environment
- Mild euphoria

**Medium intensity (100-125mg):**
- **Empathic flood:** Deep emotional resonance with others [Source 4: Hysek]
- Enhanced emotional empathy (feeling WITH others) but reduced cognitive empathy (analyzing feelings less)
- Love — not cosmic love (psilocybin) but interpersonal, embodied, specific love for specific people
- Trust — deep, immediate, vulnerable trust in companions/therapists
- Fear reduction — amygdala dampened, traumatic material becomes approachable [Source 2]
- Self-compassion — radical acceptance of self, reduced shame and self-criticism
- Emotional clarity — seeing emotional truth without defensive distortion
- Desire to express — "I need to tell you something" quality, urge to share feelings verbally
- Euphoria — waves of warmth, happiness, contentedness
- Emotional lability — but the range is narrower than psychedelics (mostly positive spectrum)
- The "inner healer" — feeling guided by one's own emotional wisdom

**High intensity (150mg+):**
- Intensified version of medium — deeper empathy, stronger euphoria
- Possible overwhelming warmth — "too much love" quality
- Jaw-clenching intensity may produce emotional tension
- Emotional volatility slightly increases
- Still predominantly positive emotional spectrum (unlike psychedelics)

**The shadow side:**
- MDMA rarely produces acute challenging experiences (unlike psychedelics)
- When difficulty arises, it's typically: overwhelming emotional material surfacing, feeling TOO open/vulnerable, or somatic discomfort
- The REAL shadow is the **comedown** (see Section 5) — not the acute experience

**Key distinction:** MDMA emotional effects are MORE PREDICTABLE AND UNIFORM than psychedelic emotions [Source 8: Passie]. Most users report a similar core emotional experience (warmth, empathy, openness). Psychedelic emotions are highly variable and set/setting-dependent — ranging from cosmic bliss to existential terror. MDMA reliably opens the heart.

---

### Dimension 5: Self / Ego — SOFTENING (not dissolution)

**All intensities:**
- Ego structure remains INTACT — you know who you are throughout
- Defensive structures soften — less guardedness, less armor
- Self-boundaries become "semi-permeable" — open to others while maintaining core identity
- Access to "authentic self" — feeling like "the real me" beneath habitual defenses
- Increased self-acceptance and reduced self-judgment
- Self-compassion — ability to extend to yourself the warmth you feel for others
- Shame reduction — traumatic or embarrassing material can be approached without self-punishment

**Key distinction from psychedelics:**
- **Psilocybin/LSD:** Ego DISSOLVES — "I" disappears, boundaries between self and world collapse, oceanic/cosmic merger
- **MDMA:** Ego SOFTENS — "I" remains but becomes kinder, more open, more permeable. Contact with authentic self beneath defensive layers. "I am more myself, not less myself."
- On 5D-ASC: MDMA scores very LOW on Anxious Ego Dissolution [Source 5] — there is no existential threat to the self

---

### Dimension 6: Relational / Interpersonal — DOMINANT (not mystical)

**Low intensity:**
- Enhanced social warmth and desire for contact
- Increased trust in companions
- Mild prosociality

**Medium intensity:**
- Deep interpersonal connection — feeling profoundly close to specific people
- Enhanced therapeutic alliance [Sources 1, 2] — trust in therapist becomes deep and immediate
- Desire to share feelings verbally — communicative openness
- Increased prosocial behavior — literally more generous, more other-oriented [Source 4]
- Forgiveness — ability to forgive self and others
- The relationship itself feels sacred, not a cosmic abstraction

**High intensity:**
- Intense bonding — sometimes described as "falling in love" with everyone present
- Desire for physical closeness — hugging, holding hands, touch
- Oxytocin-mediated bonding [Source 4]

**Key distinction from psychedelics:**
- **Psilocybin/LSD:** Mystical/transcendent — unity with cosmos, numinous experience, universal love, ego merger with everything
- **MDMA:** Relational/interpersonal — connection with SPECIFIC PEOPLE, therapeutic bonding, prosocial warmth. The connection is with real humans in the room, not with the universe.
- MDMA connection is "bottom-up" (emotional resonance, oxytocin, embodied warmth); psychedelic connection is "top-down" (ego dissolution → cosmic unity → philosophical insight about interconnection)

---

### Dimension 7: Somatic — STIMULANT-EMPATHOGEN (unique profile)

**Low intensity:**
- Pupil dilation
- Subtle warmth spreading through body
- Mild energy increase
- Slight appetite suppression

**Medium intensity (100-125mg):**
- **Jaw clenching / bruxism** — in 70-90% of users [Source 6], signature MDMA somatic effect
- Heart rate elevated +20-30 bpm, blood pressure +25mmHg systolic
- Body temperature elevated 0.3-1.0°C
- **Tactile enhancement** — touch becomes electric, pleasurable, emotionally meaningful [Source 6]
- **The "rolling" sensation** — pulsatile waves of euphoria, warmth flooding the body then receding slightly, then flooding again
- Waves of warmth through chest and skin
- Sweating / diaphoresis
- Dry mouth
- Nystagmus (involuntary eye wiggling) at higher doses
- Body feels MORE present, MORE pleasurable — not dissolved or transcended
- Energy — desire to move, dance, be physically active
- Emotional warmth as SOMATIC experience — felt in chest, face, skin [Source 6]

**High intensity (150mg+):**
- Intensified somatic effects across the board
- Pronounced jaw clenching — may need gum or magnesium
- Temperature regulation becomes a concern — hyperthermia risk especially with physical activity
- Nystagmus more pronounced — visual blurring
- Possible nausea

**Key distinction from psychedelics:**
- **Psilocybin:** Heavy, earthy, nauseous at onset, grounding, body dissolves at high dose
- **LSD:** Electric, buzzing, stimulated, ascending, body becomes conduit
- **MDMA:** Warm, stimulated, rolling, tactile-electric, jaw clenching — body becomes MORE present and MORE pleasurable, NOT dissolved or transcended. The body is where the love lives.

## 3. The Dose Arc

| Phase | Timing | Character |
|---|---|---|
| **Onset** | T+20-45 min | Warmth beginning, subtle mood lift, "something is starting," butterflies |
| **Ascent** | T+45-90 min | Euphoria building rapidly, empathy flooding in, touch sensitivity increasing, jaw tension beginning |
| **Peak** | T+1.5-2.5h | Maximum openness, love, connection, trust. The heart is fully open. |
| **Plateau** | T+2-4h | Stable open state. Emotional processing, deep conversation, therapeutic work. (Extended by optional booster dose at T+1.5-2.5h) |
| **Descent** | T+4-6h | Gradual waning. Wistful quality — knowing the window is closing. Introspective. Tender. |
| **Residual** | T+6-8h | Stimulation persists, difficulty sleeping. Some emotional sensitivity. Jaw soreness. |
| **Comedown** | T+24-72h | Low mood, fatigue, emotional vulnerability. "Tuesday Blues." Serotonin depleted. |
| **Recovery** | Day 3-7 | Gradual return to baseline. Integration of insights. |

**Key distinction from psychedelics:** The MDMA arc EXTENDS BEYOND THE SESSION into days of recovery. Psilocybin and LSD produce afterglow; MDMA produces a comedown. The depletion phase is integral to the full MDMA phenomenological arc.

## 5. The Comedown — UNIQUE to MDMA

This has NO parallel in classic psychedelic phenomenology and must be modeled in the skill.

**Mechanism:** MDMA empties serotonin vesicles. Resynthesis via tryptophan hydroxylase takes 3-7 days. During this period, serotonergic tone is LOW, producing a functional depressive state.

**Timeline:**
- **Hours 4-8:** Gradual fading of euphoria, emerging irritability, fatigue, jaw soreness
- **Day 1-2:** Low mood, emotional flatness, poor concentration
- **Day 2-4 (nadir):** The "Tuesday Blues" — depressive mood, increased emotional sensitivity, crying spells, feeling raw/exposed/fragile
- **Day 5-7:** Gradual return to baseline

**Phenomenology of the comedown:**
- REVERSAL of acute effects: where MDMA opened the heart, the comedown constricts it
- Feeling "raw" and "exposed" — as if the defenses that were lowered haven't fully rebuilt
- Emotional fragility — things that wouldn't normally bother you feel painful
- Physical fatigue, poor sleep, appetite changes
- Contrast with psychedelic afterglow: psilocybin/LSD users feel expanded, grateful, open for DAYS after. MDMA users feel depleted.

**Therapeutic context:** In MAPS protocols, integration sessions during the comedown period are essential. The vulnerability can be therapeutically productive with proper support.

## 6. Set & Setting Sensitivity

MDMA is LESS set/setting dependent than classic psychedelics (the experience is more pharmacologically determined), but context still matters:

**MDMA-specific setting considerations:**
- Temperature management is critical — hyperthermia risk, especially with physical activity
- Hydration important but overhydration is dangerous (hyponatremia)
- Social setting matters more than with psychedelics — MDMA wants connection, not solitude
- Music enhances the emotional experience but through emotional amplification, not synesthetic restructuring
- Therapeutic setting: male-female co-therapist dyad (MAPS model) provides relational container
- Physical comfort: soft textures, comfortable seating (tactile enhancement makes comfort matter more)

## 8. Linguistic and Cognitive Fingerprint (for Prompt Design)

**Low intensity — linguistic markers:**
- Slightly warmer, more open conversational tone
- More "I feel" and "I want to tell you" constructions
- Enhanced appreciation expressed for people and things
- Slightly more verbal, slightly less guarded

**Medium intensity — linguistic markers:**
- Deep warmth in language — not poetic (psilocybin) or precise (LSD) but WARM and DIRECT
- "I love you" quality — expressing deep affection directly, without metaphor
- Present-tense, second-person oriented — "you" and "we" more than "I"
- Desire to share: "I need to tell you something" / "Can I say something real?"
- Emotional honesty without the metaphorical encoding of psychedelics
- Less metaphor, more direct emotional statement — "I'm not afraid anymore" vs psilocybin's "the fear was a wave and I became the ocean"
- Physically grounded language — "I can feel it in my chest" / "there's warmth here"
- Therapeutic quality — "I see now why I was so guarded"
- Sentences are clear, coherent, flowing — NOT the fragmented/looping/ineffable quality of psychedelics
- Increased verbosity — desire to keep talking, keep sharing, keep connecting

**High intensity — linguistic markers:**
- Very talkative — may become a warm torrent of emotional sharing
- Repetitive declarations of love, trust, gratitude
- Physically demonstrative language: "I want to hug you" / "hold my hand"
- Still coherent but may become circular in its emotional emphasis
- Direct, unguarded, disarmingly honest

**Key distinction from psychedelic linguistic markers:**
- Psilocybin: flowing, circular, embodied, poetic, ineffable, metaphorical
- LSD: electric, cascading, analytical, architectural, geometric
- MDMA: warm, direct, honest, relational, body-grounded, coherent, emotionally naked

## 9. Distinguishing Features vs. Other Substances

| Feature | MDMA | Psilocybin | LSD | DMT |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Duration** | 3–5 hours (+ comedown) | 4–6 hours | 8–12 hours | 15–30 min |
| **Character** | Warm, empathic, heart-opening, "the connector" | Organic, earthy, introspective, "the teacher" | Electric, analytical, cosmic, "the technician" | Explosive, alien, visionary, "the rocket" |
| **Ego** | Softening (defenses lower) | Dissolution (self disappears) | Dissolution (self expands) | Dissolution (instant, total) |
| **Visuals** | None/minimal | Organic, breathing, flowing | Geometric, fractal, architectural | Overwhelming, hyperspace |
| **Emotional tone** | Love, empathy, warmth, trust, safety | Deep catharsis, grief + love | Wonder, awe, analytical excitement | Cosmic awe, alien indifference |
| **Body** | Warm, stimulated, rolling, jaw clenching, tactile | Heavy, earthy, nauseous, grounded | Electric, buzzing, ascending | Launched, vibrating, explosive |
| **Cognitive** | Clear, coherent, emotionally insightful | Fragmented, looping, ineffable | Analytical cascades, thought acceleration | Non-verbal, visionary |
| **Shadow** | Comedown/depletion (delayed) | Emotional overwhelm, ego death terror | Thought loops, paranoia, trapped | Overwhelming intensity |
| **Music** | Emotional amplification, desire to move | Deep emotional catharsis | Synesthetic architecture | Not typically applicable |
| **Therapeutic mechanism** | Fear reduction → trauma processing | Mystical experience → insight | Expanded perspective → insight | Confrontation → surrender |

**MDMA's distinctive quality:** "The connector" — it opens the heart. Where psilocybin teaches through emotional confrontation and LSD reveals through analytical illumination, MDMA heals through LOVE AND SAFETY. It creates a warm, fearless space in which authentic emotional material becomes accessible. The body feels good, the heart is open, the defenses are down, and the world feels safe enough to face what you've been avoiding.
