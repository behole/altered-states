# White Light
## Suno Custom Mode Prompt — 5-MeO-DMT

### Style Tags
`ambient drone, silence, white noise, meditative, sparse, formless, sacred drone, minimal, stillness, void, pure tone, Tibetan bowl, vast, wordless, infinite`

### Metatags
```
[mood: formless, absolute, beyond language, total dissolution, ineffable]
[pacing: almost no build — a single moment of dissolution, then vast silence, then quiet return]
[vocals: MINIMAL — almost no lyrics. A single word or phrase at most. Near-silence carries the piece. If any vocals, they should be whispers or breath. The silence IS the music.]
[structure: dissolution → void → return. Three sections. The void section should be the longest — mostly silence with subtle drone.]
[dynamics: near-silence throughout. A single sustained drone or tone. No beats, no melody, no rhythm. The space between sounds is more important than the sounds.]
[instrumentation: single sustained drone (sine wave or Tibetan bowl), white noise very quiet, silence as primary instrument. Nothing rhythmic. Nothing melodic. Maximum minimalism.]
[lyrical style: ALMOST NONE. This is the most lyric-sparse piece in the project. The experience is ineffable — near-total silence reflects that. Maximum 3-4 words total across the entire piece. Single syllables. Absolutes. "Home." "Gone." "Everything." If you write full sentences, you have failed.]
[CRITICAL: This is NOT N,N-DMT. No entities. No alien architecture. No chrysanthemum. No geometric tunnel. No "other place." Formless dissolution into white light / source / void. No narrative. No story. Just: gone, then back.]
```
