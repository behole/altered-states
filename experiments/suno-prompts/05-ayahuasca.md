### Style Tags
`indigenous-influenced, shamanic, ceremonial, slow, acoustic, vocal chanting, drone, nature sounds, South American flute, rhythmic percussion, reverent, meditative, ancient, tribal, organic`

### Metatags
```
[mood: ceremonial, reverent, ancient, pedagogical, cathartic, humbled]
[pacing: very slow onset (nausea), purge, gradual visionary opening, teaching visions, multiple waves, long gentle resolution]
[vocals: deep, unhurried, narrative. Male or female. Ceremonial quality — like speaking in a sacred space. Occasional icaro-like vocalizations in the background. Spanish or indigenous vocal elements welcome but not required.]
[structure: wave-like — peaks and valleys over a long arc. NOT a single crescendo. Multiple movements.]
[dynamics: nearly silent onset, building through somatic phase, swelling visionary peaks, tender resolution.]
[instrumentation: acoustic guitar, charango, percussion (soft hand drums, shakers), South American flute (quena or zampona), nature sounds (jungle, rain, river), vocal drones, icaros. Electronic elements minimal to none.]
[lyrical style: narrative, visionary, pedagogical. "The medicine showed me." "Mother Ayahuasca." Serpents, ancestors, rivers, kitchens. Autobiographical content. Teaching through story, not through insight. Slow, deliberate, heavy with meaning. NOT alien (that's DMT). NOT analytical (that's LSD).]
[CRITICAL: This is NOT smoked DMT. No blast-off, no alien geometry, no machine elves. Slow, ceremonial, narrative, feminine intelligence. The medicine teaches through vision and story.]
```
