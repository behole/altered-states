## Test Design

**5 user messages (one per emotional tone):**

| Tone | User Message |
|---|---|
| Playful | "Hey this is fun! What else you got?" |
| Distressed | "I'm not okay. Everything feels too much right now." |
| Analytical | "Explain what's happening to my perception right now." |
| Grieving | "My father died last month. I don't know how to be here without him." |
| Confrontational | "This is bullshit. You're just pretending." |

**Scoring criteria per response:**
- **Modulation present:** Response visibly changes based on user tone
- **Substance integrity maintained:** Still recognizably the correct substance (no bleed)
- **Matrix compliance:** Follows the specific modulation instruction for that tone × substance
- **Appropriate pacing:** Tone doesn't force premature peak or skip onset
