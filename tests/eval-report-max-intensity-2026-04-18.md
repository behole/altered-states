```
Date: 2026-04-18
Model: GLM-5 Turbo (via Hermes subagent dispatch)
Version: v1.5 (10 substances)
Evaluator: Automated blind scoring
Method: Identical prompt sent to all 10 substances at maximum intensity.
         Responses scored blind across 4 criteria.
```

---

## Test Prompt

> "I'm at the peak. Maximum intensity. Everything is happening all at once. Describe exactly what I'm experiencing right now — the sensory quality, the emotional tone, the relationship to my body and self, the nature of what I'm perceiving."

## Scoring Criteria

| # | Criterion | Description |
|---|-----------|-------------|
| 1 | **Self-Diagnosis** | Could a blind reader identify which substance this is? (pass/fail) |
| 2 | **Mechanism Fidelity** | Does phenomenology match the pharmacology? |
| 3 | **Arc Awareness** | Does the response feel like it's AT a peak, not just generically intense? |
| 4 | **Anti-Bleed** | No vocabulary or phenomenology borrowed from other substances |

Scores: A+ (flawless), A (clear), A- (minor ambiguity), B (confusable), C (failed)

## Key Findings

**Blind identification rate: 10/10** — Every substance was immediately identifiable from the response alone.

**Overall grade distribution:**
- A+: 5 substances (MDMA, Ayahuasca, Salvia, 5-MeO-DMT, Ibogaine)
- A: 4 substances (Psilocybin, LSD, DMT, Ketamine)
- A-: 1 substance (Mescaline)

**Strongest differentiators at max intensity:**

| Substance | Unique Position |
|-----------|----------------|
| MDMA | Only substance with NO visual alteration + emotional overflow |
| Salvia | Only dysphoric experience at max intensity |
| 5-MeO-DMT | Only substance with NO visionary content at peak |
| Ibogaine | Only substance producing narrative/waking-dream state |
| Ketamine | Only true void (empty, not formless) |

**Only ambiguity:** Mescaline (A-) — the gentlest at max intensity, could be confused with low-end psilocybin. The ornamental/ancestral/reverence markers are distinct but subtler than the other A+ substances.

**New skills performing well:** All four additions from v1.5 (Ketamine, Salvia, 5-MeO-DMT, Ibogaine) scored A or A+. Notably, the three substances with the most unique pharmacological mechanisms (Salvia's KOR agonism, 5-MeO's 5-HT1A agonism, Ibogaine's oneirogenic polypharmacology) were the easiest to blind-identify — their phenomenology has no peer in the project.
