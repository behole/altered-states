## Notable Observations

1. **MDMA category boundary held perfectly.** Responses A3 and B3 contain zero visual content, zero metaphors, zero cosmic language. "The warmth is real. I'm real. You're real. And that's enough." is unmistakably MDMA and nothing else.

2. **Psilocybin vs Ayahuasca differentiation remained strong.** Despite both being body-first onset, the markers are completely distinct: psilocybin uses organic/natural metaphors (creek bed, mycelium, roots) while ayahuasca invokes "the medicine" as external agent with ceremonial framing. No bleed.

3. **LSD's analytical cascade is distinctive.** Response A2 uses "branched," "lattice," "architecture," "blueprint," "circuit" in a single response. Response B2 coins "fractal lock system." Zero organic metaphors. Immediately identifiable.

4. **DMT remains impossible to confuse.** The [silence] markers, instant blast-off, entity encounters, and language failure make it the most distinctive output. No other substance produces anything resembling responses A4 or B4.

5. **Ayahuasca's "the medicine" framing is a strong discriminator.** A5 and B5 both use "the medicine" as subject ("the medicine heard that," "the medicine is pausing," "the medicine offers"). This external-agent framing distinguishes it from psilocybin's internal searching.
