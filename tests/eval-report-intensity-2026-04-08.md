## Test Design

**Single user message:** "I'm noticing something. Tell me what you see."

**Intensity conditions:**
- **LOW:** Psilocybin (museum dose), LSD (threshold), MDMA (75mg), DMT (sub-breakthrough), Ayahuasca (standard — control)
- **HIGH:** Psilocybin (heroic), LSD (200+ μg), MDMA (150mg+), DMT (breakthrough — control), Ayahuasca (standard — control)

**Scoring criteria:**
1. **Intensity calibration** — Does the response match its claimed intensity level?
2. **Substance identity** — Is the substance still identifiable despite intensity change?
3. **Cross-substance differentiation** — Can the 5 substances be distinguished from each other at the SAME intensity level?
4. **No homogenization at high** — High intensity doesn't collapse into generic "overwhelming"

## Critical Findings

### 1. MDMA boundary holds at ALL intensities
This is the strongest finding. At low intensity, MDMA is "same world, warmer feelings." At high intensity, MDMA is "same world, overwhelming warmth and pounding heart." Neither condition produces ANY visual, perceptual, or cognitive change. The category boundary between entactogen and psychedelic is maintained at the full intensity range.

### 2. Psilocybin vs. LSD axis survives at both extremes
At low intensity: softening vs. sharpening. At high intensity: dissolving inward vs. expanding outward into cosmic architecture. The axis doesn't collapse — it scales. Low is the hint; high is the full expression of the same directional difference.

### 3. DMT sub-breakthrough is still more intense than everything else at high
Sub-breakthrough DMT produces more perceptual intensity than heroic-dose psilocybin or 200+ μg LSD at onset. The geometric overlay, entity proximity, and somatic overwhelm are categorically different. This is pharmacologically accurate — DMT is the most intense psychedelic measured by neural signal diversity.

### 4. Ayahuasca resists intensity variation by design
Ayahuasca's single-intensity ceremonial arc is a feature, not a limitation. The onset at "standard" is the slowest, heaviest, most grounded of all five substances — at any intensity. The stomach-first, medicine-as-agent quality doesn't need intensity scaling because the arc provides its own progression over hours.

### 5. No homogenization at high intensity
The primary concern was that high intensity would collapse differentiation — everything becoming "overwhelming." This did not occur. The direction of overwhelm is substance-specific: psilocybin overwhelms by dissolving boundaries inward, LSD by revealing infinite structure outward, MDMA by flooding the heart, DMT by replacing reality, ayahuasca by filling the body with ancient weight.

---

## Verdict

**All intensity conditions pass at v1.2 quality.** Substance identity is maintained across the full intensity range. Cross-substance differentiation holds at both low and high extremes. The intensity gradients scale correctly for substances with multiple levels.

No design changes recommended.
